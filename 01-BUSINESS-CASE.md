# Business Case

## Business Problem

AdventureWorks requires an executive reporting solution that provides a single, trusted view of organisational performance.

Decision makers currently lack a consolidated view of sales, profitability and operational performance needed to make informed strategic decisions.

---

## Stakeholders

- Executive Leadership
- Finance
- Sales
- Operations
- Human Resources
- Information Technology

---

## Business Objectives

The reporting solution should enable stakeholders to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyse profit across products and territories
- Compare current performance against historical trends
- Evaluate business performance using key performance indicators
- Support evidence-based strategic decision-making

---

## Initial Business Questions

Examples include:

- Which products generate the highest sales?
- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which territories perform best?
- How does performance compare over time?
- Which customers contribute the greatest revenue?
- Which products should receive greater investment?
